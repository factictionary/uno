Brand New Mechanics

9 custom action cards that change everything

Beyond the classic UNO deck — these strategic cards introduce a whole new layer of competitive play.

⇄

SWAP

Exchange your entire hand with an opponent. High risk, high reward.

✕

PURGE

Discard every card of the chosen color from your hand in one move.

🛡

SHIELD

Block the next Draw card aimed at you. The ultimate defensive play.

↯

STEAL

Steal one random card from every opponent. They won't see it coming.

⚠

STORM

Force every opponent to draw 2 cards. Thunder rolls across the table.

⊘

ULTRA

Skip the next player AND reverse direction. A double whammy.

👁

SPY

Peek at an opponent's hand and steal one card. Information is power.

↻

AGAIN

Take another turn immediately. Chain plays without skipping a beat.

⚡

BIND

The next player must match color or draw 4. Lock them in.
